What is a (Digital) Credential?

  • "A set of one or more claims made by an issuer" (W3C Verifiable Credentials Working Group)
  • "Something that gives a title to credit or confidence" (Merriam-Webster)

Why do we need (Digital) Credentials in education & lifelong learning?

  • As milestones in a learning process
  • As beacons to guide someones learning
  • As verifiable claims that can transfer trust from learning towards practice
  • As quality assurance?
  • ...

... but are they also fit for informal recognition of informal learning?

Are Open Badges fit for informal recognition?

  • How does recognition work within communities of practice? ("do we need badges?" as Justin Mason asked)
  • How do you transfer trust without institutional trust? ("human trust as condicio sine qua non" as Sharon Leu stated)
